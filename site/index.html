About

I'm a software engineer based in Berlin, working independently on systems that need to be quietly reliable — backend services, data pipelines, and the unglamorous middleware that keeps things moving.

My background is in distributed systems and developer tooling. I take on contracts where the scope rewards careful thought over fast turnover. When I'm not at the keyboard you'll find me by the harbour with a camera, or somewhere quieter with a book.
